Visi

Misi

CIUC is called to mission with overseas students, particularly from Indonesia, to disciple future generations, and to give generous support to care for disadvantaged back home in Indonesia.

Mission Directions

  • Having a growing and strong relationship with church in Bali - Indonesia. CIUC is trying to support orphans in Bali which come from non-Christian community as it is known that most people in Bali are Hindu.
  • Supporting the Organisation for Mentally Disabled Children (Christian based) in the region of Sumatra(Indonesia) through the local church that manages the organisation.

Pendeta Lingky Hartono Widodo

Kota Melbourne dengan berbagai macam gaya hidup serta pola berpikir yang tidak sesuai dengan firman Tuhan adalah sebuah ladang yang harus dilayani dan dimenangkan untuk Kristus. Oleh sebab itu penggembalaan di Caulfield Indonesian Uniting Church membentuk wawasan hidup jemaat dengan mengajarkan firman Tuhan secara ketat sebagai dasar membangun tubuh Kristus dan hidup benar untuk melayani dan memuliakan nama Tuhan.

Pendeta Ignatius Bagoes Seta

City of Melbourne is a field that have various lifestyles and ways of thinking which contradicting God's gospel, needs to be served and won for Christ. Therefore, Caulfield Indonesian Uniting Church educate the congregates to live strictly following God's gospel as a foundation in building the body of Christ and glorifying God's name.
